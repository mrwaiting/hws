Product Description

Lose Your Post Baby Belly in Weeks

Shrinkx Belly Postpartum Belly Wrap helps you lose inches faster off your belly and waist after delivery. Shrinkx Belly is the only postpartum belly band with triple compression to customize fit and help you lose your post-baby belly in weeks instead of months.

Women around the world have been wrapping their bodies after childbirth for centuries. Postpartum belly wrapping after delivery with Shrinkx Belly helps speed weight loss and recovery.

Triple compression speeds weight loss
Supports core abdominal muscles and helps uterus return to its original size
Reduces swelling and bloating after delivery
Instantly slims so you look and feel better
Breathes easily with lightweight postpartum support made of latex free, breathable mesh fabric
Aids in pain relief as a c-section postpartum wrap
Cushions your lower back with built-in boning and improves posture while breastfeeding
Recommended by OB/GYNs
